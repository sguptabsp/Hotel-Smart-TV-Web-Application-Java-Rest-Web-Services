Aqua,Chennai

Aqua,Chennai This International restaurant is situated at a convenient location near the Chennai airport. Perched on the 8th floor of the Park Hotel at Anna Salai, Chennai this restaurant with its Mediterranean style pool side setting has won awards for its luxuriously romantic ambience and tasty food. Its menu selections range from Italian to North Indian.
