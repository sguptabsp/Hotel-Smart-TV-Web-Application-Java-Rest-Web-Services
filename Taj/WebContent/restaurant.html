Restaurants in the property

La Cantina our specialty Mexican restaurant is located by the poolside with a conventional Mexican household ambience. The restaurant serves soul-searing Mexican delicacies, variety of tequilas and stone baked pizzas.

The restaurant offers multiple seating options including cosy interiors, poolside, terrace and alfresco.

La Cantina is open from 12 pm to 3 pm and 6 pm to 12 am on the weekdays and 11 am to 12 am on the weekends.
